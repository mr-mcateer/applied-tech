00 / Teachers

Teachers: your page is here.

Five teachers teach Applied Tech across CVHS and CHS. Find your name below to see your courses and all your proficiencies in one view — or to propose an edit to anything on this site.

01Pick yourself

Click your card to open your page.

Each card links to a teacher page that concatenates every proficiency across every course you teach, deduped, grouped by course. One scroll, your scope.

02Propose an edit

Something wrong? Change it.

The canonical content lives in js/data.js in the public repo. Two ways to get an edit in.

Option A · GitHub PR

Clone, edit, open a pull request.

If you’re comfortable with git: clone mr-mcateer/applied-tech, edit js/data.js, and open a PR. You can also use the edit-in-browser link and GitHub will make the branch for you.

Option B · Email Andy

Send the change in plain English.

If git isn’t your thing: andy.mcateer@corvallis.k12.or.us . The subject line is pre-filled — add the course or teacher slug after the colon.

Every edit shows up in the commit log, so there’s an auditable trail of who changed what and when.
