03Questions

A few things students ask.

Can I switch pathways? +

Yes. The 9th-grade courses keep your options open, and most upper-level courses don’t have hard prerequisites. If you decide sophomore or junior year that metals fits better than CS, talk to your counselor — they’ll help you slot in.

Do I have to pick in 9th grade? +

No. You pick a pathway in 10th grade. Freshman year is Woods 1, Metals 1, or Computer Skills — all three are broad on purpose so you can try things before you commit.

Can I take classes at the other school? +

Yes, with scheduling help from your counselor. A lot of kids do — a CVHS student who wants Metals II or the CS ladder takes it at CHS, and vice versa. Bell times and transport are real, so plan it with your counselor early.
