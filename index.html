00 / Applied Tech

Start here. Pick where you want to go.

Applied Tech isn’t a major — it’s a set of year-long courses you pick à la carte at Crescent Valley and Corvallis High. Every student starts with one of three freshman courses, then assembles a 10th–12th grade plan around where they want to end up.

20 courses  ·  11 career destinations  ·  5 teachers  ·  2 buildings

01Start here · 9th grade

Every student starts with one of these three.

The only locked prerequisite in the whole program is the freshman row: pick at least one. You can take all three if you want to try everything. They open up every pathway and every career below.

02Then pick where you want to go

Pick a career. Watch the courses that help you get there light up.

Click any career below and the Applied Tech courses that point there will highlight on the map. Bright outline = a must-have course for that career; soft outline = a supporting course that strengthens the path. Click it again to clear and see the full catalog.
